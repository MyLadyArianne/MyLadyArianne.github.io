Tank Animation

Project: Create a animation about a tank.
Team: Individual project
Date: November-December 2015, 8 week project
Tasks: Rig, animate and create environment.
Programs: Maya, Houdini.
Description: A shot animation about a little tank overcoming a big problem. Basic tank model was provided by a teacher, and to us the task of rigging and animating it. We had to come up with the details of the story and make an animatic with it. The environment was created in Houdini, though it was a very simple environment I learned the very basics of Houdini with it. Then the rig for the tank which I made in Maya. I had to make the parts for the rotating tracks myself, since they were part of my solution to the problem and not part of the original model.And the final animation was also made in Maya.
