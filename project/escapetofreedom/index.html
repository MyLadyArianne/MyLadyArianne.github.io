Escape to freedom

Project: Create a shot animation with the theme Escape to freedom.
Team: Individual project
Date: September-October 2016, 8 week project
Tasks: Animated the main Hare character.
Programs: Maya, Primiere Pro.
Description: For this animation my pet rabbits were my inspiration. They don't want to escape because they don't like their cage, it is more like they find the world around them much more interesting and want to explore. The grass always looks greener on the other side of the fence. Same for this Hare the green grass looks much more tempting than his bare cage. To emphasize I out the environment together with a lot of color outside his cage as for the inside where I used gray-brownish colors. I first created some storyboards, once they looked promising I went on to the an animatic and then onto the actual animation.
