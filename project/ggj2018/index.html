Global Game Jam 2018

Project: Global Game Jam 2018
Team: Team Project: 3 Programmers, 2 Artists and 2 Designers
Date: Feb 2018, 48 hours
Tasks: Create environment assets, rig and animate characters.
Programs: Maya, Substance painter, Photoshop, Unreal.
Description: We created this game during the global game jam of 2018, inspired by the theme: Transmission. In abDUCKted you mind control a duck through transmission towers to guide him through the level. The duck receives the transmitted signals through the big antenna on top of his head. The bigger idea behind this is that the aliens on top of the towers are trying to abduct the duck for mysterious reasons.
