Illusion Project

Project: Ilsuion Project
Team: Individual
Date: September-October 2015, 8 week project.
Tasks: Model, texture and render
Programs: Maya, Photoshop.
Description: For this project we had to model a house. Sounds simple but the was a big mistake in the drawing of the house. It seems to be made out a whole house but in reality, this house could not possibly exist out of just one piece. We had to figure out how to solve this problem and model, texture and render the house in Maya. The original drawing of the house was provided by a teacher.

The house from the camera angle it seems whole and one from were you can clearly see it is not.

Part of the exercise included drawing the illusion in a perspective view, orthographic view and an isometric view from the other side.
