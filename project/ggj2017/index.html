Global Game Jam 2017

Project: Create a game in 48 hours with the theme: Waves.
Team: Team Project with 10 people.
Date: Feb 2017, 48 hours
Tasks: Mainly Texturing
Programs: Maya, Unity, Photoshop.
Description: We created this game during the global game jam of 2017, inspired by the theme: Waves. In nimby (Not In My Backyard) you use air waves to blow the leaves from your garden into your neighbours garden. This of course is a multiplayer game and the winner is the player that ends up with the least leaves in their garden.
